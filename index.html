Aaron White's Biography

Aaron White

Former Head of Archeaology at Iria University

Portrait of Professor White painted by a colleague on the eve of his 60th birthday.

Biographical timeline of Dr. White's life:

  • 500 - Born to Augustus and Miriam White in Adeluna
  • 504 - Family immigrated across the Nyella Ocean to Iria
  • 505 - Attended first classes at Iria University's junior division
  • 515 - Graduated into post secondary coursework
  • 520 - Received doctorate in archaeology at exceptionally young age and began interning in the Department of Magical Obscurities
  • 530 - Promoted to Senior Archaeologist after 10 years of service.
  • 532 - Suddenly forced to assume the role of department director after the former director and other senior staff are wiped out by a small, otherwise innocuous looking artifact.
  • 545 - Dr. White finds the box that killed his predecessor and managed to contain it.
  • 546 - Dr. White promotes the Associate Roderick who helped him contain Anomaly 532 the previous year.
  • 559 - White starts being pressured to retire after a successful career.
  • 560 - Rumors abound that White and Roderick's relationship has strained after several heated arguments
  • 561 - White begins making frequent trips to Ataiyo, the kingdom to the north, bringing with him study materials to purpotedly teach a gifted individual.
  • 573 - Dr. White receives a letter of distress from department colleagues calling him to a secret excavation site in Kurayo
  • Dec. 573 - White makes one last trip to Ataiyo and then goes onto Kurayo
  • June 574 - After 6 months of no word, a search party is sent out. The excavation site, an old mansion with an interesting history, has vanished without a trace. The professor, along with his associates, are presumed dead.

Before you think me a genius, Roderick, you should take a look outside. All of those children have the potential to be prodigies. I refuse to believe it's some inherent gift bestowed by the gods at birth. That's the sort of tripe you hear in the west.

Aaron White shortly before his repeated trips to Ataiyo
